Our Industry Specialisation

At RoadWatch, we specialize in the intersection of technology and public safety, bringing innovative solutions to the traffic monitoring and road safety industry. Our expertise lies in deploying AI-driven tools, IoT-enabled smart devices, and real-time data analytics to address critical challenges like traffic violations, accident detection, and predictive traffic management. We are dedicated to transforming road safety with technology that not only supports law enforcement but also empowers communities to experience safer, smarter transportation environments.

OUR TECHNOLOGY 01

At RoadWatch, we harness advanced technologies to transform road safety and traffic monitoring. Our solution incorporates YOLO (You Only Look Once) for real-time object detection, allowing the identification of multiple traffic violations, such as speeding and helmet compliance, as they occur. Complementing this, Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) enhance the accuracy of object recognition by learning from extensive datasets. Our smart helmets, equipped with IoT devices, continuously gather real-time environmental data, contributing to both individual and public safety. With cloud integration, we efficiently store and process vast amounts of data, enabling seamless access to geotagged violations and fostering collaboration with law enforcement. Additionally, our use of data analytics and artificial intelligence helps predict traffic patterns, offering actionable insights for improved traffic management. Together, these technologies create a robust foundation for a safer and smarter transportation system.
